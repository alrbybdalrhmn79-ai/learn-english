TalkFluent was born from the frustration of traditional language learning methods that focus too heavily on grammar rules and vocabulary lists. We noticed that many learners could read and write English well, but struggled with natural, flowing conversations.

Drawing from real-world experience and extensive research into effective language acquisition, we created a resource specifically designed to bridge that gap between textbook knowledge and conversational fluency.
