Basic Greetings:

  • Hi/Hello - Casual greeting
  • Good morning/afternoon/evening - Time-specific greeting
  • How are you? - Follow up: "I'm fine, thanks!"
  • Nice to meet you - When meeting someone new

Introductions:

  • "My name is..." or "I'm..."
  • "This is my friend/colleague..."
  • "Where are you from?" ‚Üí "I'm from..."
  • "What do you do?" ‚Üí "I'm a student/teacher/doctor..."

‚úàÔ∏è Travel & Transportation

At the Airport:

  • "Where is the gate for flight ___?"
  • "I'd like to check in my luggage"
  • "What time does the flight depart?"
  • "Is there a delay?"

Getting Directions:

  • "How do I get to ___?"
  • "Is it far from here?"
  • "Can you show me on the map?"
  • "Which bus/train should I take?"

At Hotels:

  • "I'd like to book a room for ___ nights"
  • "Do you have WiFi?"
  • "What's the checkout time?"

üíº Workplace Communication

Meeting Starters:

  • "Let's get started" or "Shall we begin?"
  • "I'd like to discuss..."
  • "Does anyone have any questions?"
  • "Let's take a break" or "We'll continue tomorrow"

Email Phrases:

  • "Dear ___," (formal) / "Hi ___," (casual)
  • "I'm writing to..." or "I hope this email finds you well"
  • "Please find attached..."
  • "Best regards," or "Thanks,"

Office Small Talk:

  • "How was your weekend?"
  • "Did you watch the game last night?"
  • "How's the project going?"
  • "Are you free for lunch?"

üõçÔ∏è Shopping & Dining

At Stores:

  • "How much does this cost?"
  • "Do you have this in a different size/color?"
  • "Can I try this on?"
  • "I'll take it" or "That's all, thank you"

At Restaurants:

  • "A table for ___, please"
  • "What's today's special?"
  • "I'd like to order ___" or "Can I have the menu?"
  • "Could I have the bill/check, please?"

Food Descriptions:

  • "I'd like something spicy/mild"
  • "No ___ please" (nuts, dairy, etc.)
  • "Can you make it to go?"

üéâ Casual Conversations

Starting Conversations:

  • "Beautiful weather today, isn't it?"
  • "Have you been here before?"
  • "What brings you here?"
  • "That's a nice ___ you have"

Keeping it Going:

  • "Really? Tell me more about that"
  • "That's interesting! What happened next?"
  • "I feel the same way" or "I know what you mean"
  • "Have you tried ___?"

Expressing Opinions:

  • "I think..." or "In my opinion..."
  • "I agree/disagree because..."
  • "That's a good point"
  • "I'm not sure, what do you think?"

üë• Social Situations

Making Plans:

  • "Are you free this weekend?"
  • "Would you like to ___?"
  • "What time works for you?"
  • "Sounds good! See you then"

Expressing Gratitude:

  • "Thank you so much!"
  • "I really appreciate it"
  • "That was very kind of you"
  • "I owe you one"

Apologizing:

  • "I'm sorry for..."
  • "I apologize for the inconvenience"
  • "That was my fault"
  • "How can I make it up to you?"
